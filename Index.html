About SUT

Sovex University of Technology (SUT) has established itself as a premier institution in Nigeria, distinguished by its excellence in technology, research, and development. Since its inception in 2018, SUT has been committed to fostering a learning environment that empowers students to harness their skills and innovative capacities to address pressing contemporary challenges. Through its rigorous academic programs and state-of-the-art infrastructure.

The university aims to produce graduates who are not only industry-ready but also globally competitive, thereby contributing to the advancement of society.
